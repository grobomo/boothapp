# Trade-Show Quality HTML Report

## Goal
Upgrade render-report.js and render-report.html to produce a trade-show quality HTML report with company logo placeholder, visitor photo placeholder, large section headers, executive summary box, product list with icons, engagement score gauge, timeline with screenshots, and action items as cards.

## Success Criteria
1. Company logo placeholder in header (image placeholder with fallback)
2. Visitor photo placeholder (avatar/badge photo area)
3. Large, visually prominent section headers
4. Executive summary in a styled callout box
5. Product list with category icons
6. Engagement score as a visual gauge (not just number)
7. Timeline entries support screenshot thumbnails
8. Action items rendered as styled cards with priority indicators
9. All existing tests pass
10. Report looks like a professional sales follow-up document
